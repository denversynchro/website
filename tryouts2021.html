Skills Sessions & Tryouts for 2020/2021

  • All skills sessions and tryouts will be held at The Edge Ice Arena.

  • All skaters must pre-register using the following links:
    • Register and pay for skaters under 18
    • Register and pay for adult and masters skaters 18+
  • Participation cost is $75 which includes registration, skills sessions and tryouts.

  • Participation in skills sessions is highly recommended for skaters planning to attend tryouts.
  • Tryout elements and expectations will be taught at each skills session.
  • Individual tryouts are permitted if a skater is unable to attend any skills or tryout sessions.

  • Contact info@denversynchro.org for more information.
